Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click "Create Account", fill in your details, and submit. For admin accounts, you need a special admin key.
Is the fee clearance system secure?
Yes, all payment proofs are reviewed manually, and receipts include QR verification.
Can lecturers use Njala Connect?
Yes, verified lecturers can broadcast announcements and create class groups.
